				<p><b>PSYCHOSOCIAL DONOR EVALUATION</b></p>
<p>Following questions are mostly involved in a psychosocial donor evaluation:</p>
<p><b>A.  DECISION TO DONATE</b></p>
<p>What is your understanding of the recipient&#8217;s illness and why a transplant is needed?</p>
<p>When and how did the subject of donation arise?</p>
<p>What was the recipient&#8217;s reaction to your offer?</p>
<p>What are your family&#8217;s feelings about your being a donor?</p>
<p>How did you arrive at the decision to be a donor?</p>
<p>How would your family and friends react if you decided not to be a donor?</p>
<p>How would you feel if you cannot be the donor for any reason?</p>
<p>What is your relationship to the recipient?</p>
<p>How will your relationship with the recipient change if you donate your kidney?</p>
<p>Will your being a donor affect any other relationships in your life?</p>
<p><b>B. TRANSPLANT ISSUES</b></p>
<p>Do you have an understanding of the process of transplant?</p>
<p>Do you understand the risk of rejection of your kidney by the recipient at some point after transplant?</p>
<p>Have you thought about how you might feel if the kidney/liver is rejected?</p>
<p>Do you have any doubts or concerns about donating?</p>
<p>Do you understand that there will be pain and soreness after the transplant?</p>
<p>What are your expectations about your recuperation?</p>
<p>Do you need to speak further to any of the transplant team members?</p>
<p><b>C.  MEDICAL HISTORY</b></p>
<p>What previous illnesses or surgeries have you had? </p>
<p>Are you currently on any medications?</p>
<p>Have you ever spoken with a counselor, a therapist or a psychiatrist?</p>
<p>Do you smoke?</p>
<p>In a typical week, how many drinks do you consume?  What drink do you prefer?</p>
<p>What kinds of recreational drugs have you tried?  Have you used any recently?</p>
<p><b>D. FAMILY AND SUPPORT SYSTEM</b></p>
<p>With whom do you live?  </p>
<p>If you are in a relationship:</p>
<p>- length of the relationship:  <br />- name of spouse/partner:  <br />- age and health of spouse/partner:  <br />- children:  </p>
<p><b>E. POST-SURGICAL PLANS</b></p>
<p>With whom will you stay after discharge?  </p>
<p>What is your current occupation:  </p>
<p>Do you have the support of your employer?     <br />- paid leave:  <br />- disability coverage:  <br />- supportive environment for your absence:  </p>
<p>Will you be able to afford any expenses you incur?<br />- travel:  <br />- lodging:  <br />- medication:  <br />- child care:  <br />- ongoing bills at home:  </p>
<p><b>F.  ASSESSMENT</b></p>
<p><b>G.  RECOMMENDATION</b></p>
