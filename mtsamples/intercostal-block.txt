<p><b>POSTOPERATIVE DIAGNOSIS: </b> Severe post thoracotomy pain.</p>
<p><b>PROCEDURE: </b> Intercostal block, left.</p>
<p><b>PROCEDURE DETAIL: </b> With the patient in the ICU bed who was having a large amount of intravenous narcotic to control his thoracotomy pain, after obtaining informed consent, his left posterior chest was prepped and draped in the usual fashion and Marcaine 0.025% was injected in the spaces four to eight sequentially.  A total of 40 mL of Marcaine was used.</p>
<p>The patient tolerated the procedure well and experienced immediate benefit out of the procedure.</p>
