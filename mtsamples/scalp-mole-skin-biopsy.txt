				<p><b>PROCEDURE: </b> Skin biopsy, scalp mole.</p>
<p><b>INDICATION:  </b>A 66-year-old female with pulmonary pneumonia, effusion, rule out metastatic melanoma to lung.</p>
<p><b>PROCEDURE NOTE: </b> The patient&#8217;s scalp hair was removed with:<br />1.  K-Y jelly.<br />2.  Betadine prep locally.<br />3.  A 1% lidocaine with epinephrine local instilled.<br />4. <p><b>IMPRESSION:</b>  Darkened mole status post punch biopsy, scalp lesion, rule out malignant melanoma with pulmonary metastasis.</p>
<p><b>PLAN: </b> The patient will have sutures removed in 10 days.</p>
