				<p><b>PROCEDURE:  </b>Caudal epidural steroid injection without fluoroscopy.</p>
<p><b>ANESTHESIA:</b>  Local sedation.</p>
<p><b>VITAL SIGNS: </b> See nurse&#8217;s records.</p>
<p><b>PROCEDURE DETAILS: </b> INT was placed.  The patient was in the prone position.  The back was prepped with Betadine.  Lidocaine 1.5% was used to make a skin wheal over the sacral hiatus.  A 18-gauge Tuohy needle was then placed into the epidural space.  There were no complications from this (no blood or CSF).  After negative aspiration was performed, a mixture of 10 cc preservative free normal saline plus 160 mg preservative free Depo-Medrol was injected.  Neosporin and band-aid were applied over the puncture site.  The patient was then placed in supine position.  The patient was discharged to outpatient recovery in stable condition.</p>
