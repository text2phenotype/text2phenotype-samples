				<p><b>EXAM: </b> Modified barium swallow.</p>
<p><b>SYMPTOM:</b>  Dysphagia with possible aspiration.</p>
<p><b>FINDINGS:</b>  A cookie deglutition study was performed.  The patient was examined in the direct lateral position.</p>
<p>Patient was challenged with thin liquids, thick liquid, semisolids and solids.</p>
<p>Persistently demonstrable is the presence of penetration with thin liquids.  This is not evident with thick liquids, semisolids or solids.</p>
<p>There is weakness in the oral phase of deglutition.  Subglottic region appears normal.  There is no evidence of aspiration demonstrated.</p>
<p><b>IMPRESSION: </b> Penetration demonstrated with thin liquids with weakness of the oral phase of deglutition.</p>
